إعداد وتنسيق وشرح: مستر/ محمود أبوهند – مع تمنياتي لكم بالتوفيق والنجاح
★ما معنى التصريف الأول والثاني والثالث؟
كل فعل في الإنجليزية له 3 صور: الأول = المصدر (نستخدمه في المضارع وبعد will / should / Let's...) | الثاني = الماضي (مع yesterday / last week) | الثالث = نستخدمه بعد have / has.
✅ أفعال منتظمة: نضيف ed فقط في الثاني والثالث — وهي الأسهل.

1should • Let's • Why don't you • will • would • could • can • must • going to
بعدها فعل بدون أي إضافات (المصدر)
Let's play football.
You should eat healthy food.
Would you pass me the salt?
I will meet you at seven.
Yasser is going to eat pizza.
2go / goes
الفعل بعدها يأخذ ing
Ali goes swimming three times a week.
Let's go shopping.
3than
اختر الصفة التي بها er، وللصفة الطويلة more، وتذكر: good → better
Jaber is taller than Hatem.
Jeddah is more modern than Abha.
Ali's mark is better than Hassan's.
4the + أفضل واحد في الكل
اختر الصفة التي بها est، وللصفة الطويلة most، وتذكر: good → the best
Bushra is the tallest student in the class.
Riyadh is the most modern city in KSA.
Dr Omar is the best doctor in the city.
5so / such / so many / so much
بعدها صفة فقط → so | صفة + اسم → such | اسم جمع → so many | اسم لا يُعد (money / salt) → so much
The meal was so delicious that we ate too much.
John is such a naughty boy that nobody likes him.
I have so many watches.
He put so much salt.
6my / mine • her / hers • their / theirs
الفراغ وبعده اسم (book / house) → my / her / their | الفراغ في آخر الجملة بدون اسم → mine / hers / theirs
It is their house. بعدها اسم
This house is theirs. بدون اسم
That is not her book. — The book is hers.
7one / ones / too
الشيء مفرد → one | جمع → ones | قبل صفة بمعنى (أكثر من اللازم) → too
Which coat? – The green one. مفرد
Which boots? – The black ones. جمع
They were too expensive.
8want • ask • tell + شخص
بعد الشخص يأتي to + فعل
I want Omar to take out the garbage.
Ask him to bring some snacks.
Tell the children to lower their voices.
9was / were
الفعل بعدها يأخذ ing
When the phone rang, she was writing a letter.
They were playing golf.
10have / has / haven't / hasn't
بعدها التصريف الثالث | He / She / مفرد → has، والباقي → have
He has already played computer games.
They haven't eaten the lunch yet.
Have they climbed a mountain?
11yesterday • last week • ago
الزمن ماضٍ بسيط (التصريف الثاني)
Eman ate a whole pizza yesterday.
The students wrote a test last week.
12now • at the moment
am / is / are + فعل ing (مضارع مستمر)
The students are writing a test at the moment.
I am studying now.
13usually • always • every day / month
مضارع بسيط (الفعل الأساسي بدون إضافات، ومع He/She نضيف s)
The students usually write a test every month.
He always plays football on Friday.
14yet • so far • already • ever • never
have / has + التصريف الثالث (مضارع تام)
The students have written a test so far.
Talal hasn't repaired his bike yet.
15If ...
بعد if مضارع بسيط دائماً (ممنوع will بعد if) | والجزء الآخر فيه will / won't / may + فعل
If it rains, they won't play tennis.
If you study hard, you will pass your exam.
If you put the water in the freezer, it becomes ice. حقيقة علمية: الجزءان مضارع
16have to / has to / had to
I, You, We, They → have to | He, She → has to | جملة في الماضي (yesterday) → had to
I have to study for a test.
He has to clean his room.
Yesterday I had to look after my baby.
17must / mustn't
أمر واجب وصحيح → must | أمر خاطئ وممنوع → mustn't
You must listen to your parents.
You mustn't cheat.
18lend / borrow
lend = تعطي غيرك (يُعير) | borrow = تأخذ من غيرك (يستعير)
Will you lend me your camera?
Can I borrow your pen?
19request طلب / offer عرض
يبدأ بسؤال Will / Would / Could you...? → طلب | يبدأ بـ I'll أو Let me → عرض
Will you help me, please? طلب
I'll help you. عرض
Let me carry that for you. عرض
